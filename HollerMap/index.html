HollerMap

Manual

Installation

Download HollerMap and execute it. Follow the instructions.

First of all you have to configure the program. In order to acheive this, launch HollerMapConfigure in "Windows Menu">Programs>HollerMap. Theb follow these steps :

  • A white screen appears, move your pen on the screen to touch each of the side of the screen. Then press any key.
  • Select the directories where you want the files to be exported.

Usage

Launch "Windows Menu">Programs>HollerMap>HollerMap. Two panel appers. The drawing panel a full screen white frame with horizontal lines and the grapth panel allowing you manage the program.
